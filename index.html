How It Works
01

Create an Account

Sign up with your email and choose a role. Guests can upload and view. Admins get full management access.

02

Upload Your Media

Go to the Upload tab, enter your Event ID and Event Name, then drag and drop your photos or videos.

03

Share the Event ID

Give your Event ID to anyone you want to invite. They use the Join Event tab to find and view all media for that event.

04

Browse and Download

View the full gallery, search by event name or title, and download any photo or video at any time.
